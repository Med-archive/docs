1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Med archive ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by an individual developer in Ukraine, collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected in compliance with the GDPR, CCPA, and the Law of Ukraine "On Protection of Personal Data."

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us:

Account Data: Name/Username, Email Address, Phone Number, Age, and Password.

Authentication Data: If you use Google or Facebook Login, we receive basic profile information authorized by those providers (e.g., name, email).

User Content: Any medical documents, images, or data you choose to upload via the Camera or Photo Gallery.

3. Device Permissions

To provide specific features, the app requests access to:

Camera: To allow you to scan or take photos of medical records.

Photo Gallery: To allow you to upload existing documents or images.

Note: We do not use Geolocation, Contacts, or background tracking.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data strictly to:

  • Create and maintain your account.
  • Provide the "archive" functionality (storage and retrieval).
  • Communicate with you regarding account support.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

We do not use your data for advertising or retargeting.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data based on:

Consent: You explicitly agree to these terms when signing up.

Contractual Necessity: To provide the service you requested.

Special Category Data: Since your uploads may contain health information, we process this data only with your explicit action (uploading).

6. Data Storage and Security

Storage: Your data is stored securely. Given the nature of medical archives, we use industry-standard encryption.

International Transfers: As the developer is based in Ukraine, your data may be processed outside the EEA. We ensure adequate protection measures are in place.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

Access & Portability: Request a copy of your data.

Correction: Update inaccurate info.

Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request that we delete your account and all associated records.

Withdraw Consent: Stop us from processing data by deleting your account.

8. Children's Privacy

Med archive is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact: medarchiveapp@gmail.com.
